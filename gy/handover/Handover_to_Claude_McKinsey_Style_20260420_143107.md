**To**: Claude (AI Presentation Designer)

**Subject**: Re-engineering the Deck for "McKinsey-Standard" Visual Authority

## 1. Core Visual/Logic Philosophy (The "McKinsey Way")
Based on the Shanxi Securities benchmark, we must move away from "explaining the system" and toward "proving the value." 

**The Golden Rules for v3.1**:
- **Action Titles ONLY**: Every title must be a full, punching conclusion.
- **Left/Right Vertical Labels**: Use sidebar labels to categorize content (e.g., 愿景, 现状, 挑战).
- **The "Insight Box" (核心启示)**: Every slide must have a dedicated callout box on the right or bottom labeled "对国元的启示".

---

## 2. Slide-by-Slide Refinement Directives

### Slide 2: 执行摘要 (Executive Summary)
- **Review**: Too much text in tables.
- **Suggestion**: Use a **"Horizontally Split Vision Box"**. Top: 战略目标 (Low Vol 6%). Bottom: 三大支柱 (System + Data + Process). 
- **Callout**: Highlight the "灯塔效应" (Lighthouse Effect) as the primary short-term goal.

### Slide 3-4: 业务现状 (Current State)
- **Review**: Standard lists are boring. 
- **McKinsey Fix**: Use a **"Comparison Waterfall"** or a **"Maturity Grid"**. 
- **Action Title**: "国元自营已跨入‘去方向性’深水区，现有IT基建已成战略瓶颈".
- **Insight Box**: "牌照已就位，但驾驶舱（POMS）缺失导致资产收益无法穿透归因".

### Slide 6: 924 事件 (The Crisis Case)
- **Review**: The table is informative but lacks "Punch."
- **McKinsey Fix**: Use a **"Timeline of Pain"**. Show a clock ticking. 
- **Visual**: A large, red callout bubble: "**1.2亿损失差额**". 
- **Insight**: "924当天致命的是流动性评估真空，POMS的实时冲击成本计算是避免再次‘踏空’的唯一路径".

### Slide 7: 投资经理的一天 (The PM's Day)
- **Review**: Good logic, needs cleaner visual comparison.
- **McKinsey Fix**: Use two distinct boxes side-by-side with icons. 
- **Key Concept**: Add the **"Institutionalization of Knowledge" (知识固化)** icon. Explain that Star PMs' logic stays in the system even if they leave.

### Slide 14: 生态集成 (Ecosystem)
- **Review**: The architecture diagram is too technical.
- **McKinsey Fix**: Use the **"Auto-pilot" Metaphor** visually. 
- **Visual**: Bottom layer (Engine) = IBOR. Middle layer (Sensors) = Market Data. Top layer (Autopilot/Brain) = POMS.
- **Action Title**: "POMS作为‘业务智能层’，旨在盘活而非替代现有数据资产".

### Slide 17: ROI 价值 (The Money Slide)
- **Review**: Numbers are lost in the text.
- **McKinsey Fix**: Use a **"Value Waterfall Chart"**. 
- **Strict Logic**: Label the "0.02% Alpha" as the "底线假设" (Bottom-line Assumption) to prove robustness.
- **Insight**: "即使系统只发挥1/10的行业均值效能，年化回报已远超建设投入".
