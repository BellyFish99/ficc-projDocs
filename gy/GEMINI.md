# Project: Guoyuan Securities FICC & POMS Platform Strategy

This project focuses on the strategic planning and solution design for Guoyuan Securities' (国元证券) FICC (Fixed Income, Currencies, and Commodities) platform and POMS (Portfolio and Order Management System).

## Contextual Mandates
- **Terminology**: Always use professional financial and trading terminology (e.g., FICC, POMS, IFRS9, FVTPL/FVOCI/AC, TCA, Multi-asset, Multi-strategy).
- **Organization Name**: Refer to the client as "国元证券" or "国元" in documents.
- **Strategic Focus**: Prioritize multi-asset investment capabilities, risk management (liquidity, compliance, market risk), and operational efficiency (automated rebalancing, TCA).
- **Consistency**: Ensure all new content aligns with the latest iterations of the solution skeleton (currently `solution_skeleton_v6.md`) and the core pain points identified in `key_values.txt`.
- **Compliance & Accounting**: Pay special attention to IFRS9 accounting standards and real-time compliance pre-checks.

## Document Standards
- **Tone**: Professional, analytical, and strategy-oriented (management consulting style).
- **Structure**: Follow the structure defined in `solution_structure.txt` for strategy documents:
    1. Strategic Planning Summary
    2. Alignment with Industry Trends
    3. Deep Demand Analysis
    4. Proposed Solutions
    5. Implementation Roadmap
- **Language**: Primary language for end-user documents is Chinese, while technical or internal notes may be in English or a mix.

## Key Project Files
- `key_values.txt`: Core business pain points and value propositions.
- `solution_skeleton_v*.md`: Iterative drafts of the solution proposal.
- `solution_structure.txt`: High-level document outline.
- `CEO_Deck_v*.md`: Executive-level presentation content.

## Workflow Preferences
- When updating skeletons, maintain the versioning pattern (e.g., incrementing `v6` to `v7`).
- Cross-reference new suggestions with the 16 specific pain points listed in `key_values.txt`.
